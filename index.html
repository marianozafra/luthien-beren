Luthien and Beren

Luthien

Luthien, fairest and most beautiful of all the children of Iluvatar.

Luthien, whose voice was sweeter than any bird or harp.

Luthien, whose love for Beren knew no bounds.

Beren

Beren, son of Barahir, last heir of the House of Beor.

Beren, who defied the power of Morgoth and dared to steal a Silmaril.

Beren, who loved Luthien with all his heart and soul.
